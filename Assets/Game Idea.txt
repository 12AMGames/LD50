Wario ware inspired game about delaying death.

There are 2 things that are inevitable in life,
and taxes got you years ago, and now
death is after you too.

30 sec game length
5 mini games or so
Game 1: Throw cats at death to distract him
Game 2: Death try's to hit you with a car
Game 3: Space invaders syle mini game with bullet hell element
Game 4: Travel across water by clicking to bail water and keep it within a gauge
Game 5: Click skull rockets
Game 6: Arm wrestle death by clicking as fast as posibble
Game 7: Choose to eat food on a conveyer belt, some of the food is bad
Final Game: a bunch of these 

In the afterlife you talk to death
